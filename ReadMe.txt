Rocket League Battle Cards Stream Assistant\

This program was made by JustSpirit to streamline the process of playing RLBC on stream or personally. This can be played online or local. 

To play online just go to {\field{\*\fldinst{HYPERLINK "https://imjustspirit.github.io/RLBCSA/"}}{\fldrslt ImJustSpirit.github.io/RLBCSA}}.

To play local:
1. Download {\field{\*\fldinst{HYPERLINK "https://github.com/ImJustSpirit/RLBCSA/archive/main.zip"}}{\fldrslt this folder}}.\
2. Run index.html.
